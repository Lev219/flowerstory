⁕CHRYSANTHEMUMS⁕

I'm really love to send myself flowers. Instead of expecting someone to send me something, I am much happier to buy what I like instead. I usually choose 1-2 flowers of each type. I like the feeling of small but delicate. Of course the day I buy flowers is very random. I remember it was winter and I chose pink and white gradient chrysanthemums. I had never seen this kind of flower before and I thought it was very special. I gave it to myself as a match with pink lavender.

我是一个很喜欢送自己花的人。与其期待别人送我什么，我按照自己的喜好去买反而更加幸福。我一般每个品种的花就会选1-2只。我喜欢小但精致的感觉。 当然买花的日子是很随机的。记得那时是冬天，我选的是粉色白色渐变的菊花。我从来没见过这种花，我觉得很特别。我把它和粉色的薰衣草作为搭配送给自己。
