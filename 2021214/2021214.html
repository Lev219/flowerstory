᪥ 33 ROSES ᪥

33 roses for my dear mother on valentines day, doing dads job for him. With the number 33 symbolising 3 eternities love and affection and the vibrant orange roses implying youth and beauty. The flowers encapsulate her energy and excitement towards life, while being wrapped elegantly in black paper like the sophisticated woman she presents to the world.

在情人节为我亲爱的母亲送上33朵玫瑰，算是替爸爸完成他的工作。数字33象征着3个永恒的爱情和亲情，充满活力的橙色玫瑰意味着年轻和美丽。这些花概括了她的能量和对生活的兴奋，同时用黑纸优雅地包裹着，就像她向世界展示的成熟女人。
