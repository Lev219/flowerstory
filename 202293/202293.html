$$RICH$$

This is a bouquet of flowers I gave my mum for her birthday. I chose her favourite florist. This florist often brings in rare flowers.

The Chinese believe that yellow signifies money and also means wealth. So I specifically asked the florist to find me some Japanese Garland lilies. Lilies is one of my mum's favourite flowers. I remember when I was a child she used to buy lilies to put in all kinds of fancy vases.

This was the first time I gave my mum a bouquet in yellow and red tones. The overall effectiveness was really nice. It gives a very powerful feeling. It also symbolises what she means to me inside.

这是我在生日的时候送给妈妈的一束花。我选择了她最喜欢的花店。这家花店经常会引进一些稀有花材。 中国人认为黄色寓意着金钱，同时也有发财的意思。所以我特地吩咐花店帮我找点日本的嘉兰百合。百合是妈妈最喜欢的花之一。记得小时候她经常会买百合放在各种好看的花瓶里。 这是我第一次送妈妈黄色系和红色系的花束。整体成效真的很好看。给人很有力量的感觉。这也象征着他她在我内心的含义。
