WELCOMEING SPRING

This is the Women's Day bouquet I gave to my mom this year. This is the first time I have given my mom a bouquet with such bright colors. The color combination of this bouquet came from my recent conversation with her-Although mom is in her 50s, but she seems to be back to my age recently. I was happy for her from the bottom of my heart. For me, age is just a number. It's how you live your life and make every day in joy that counts. This bouquet is not only to celebrate the state of my mom's life lately, but I hope this bouquet will make her joyful to welcome the spring. The first bouquet of flowers to welcome spring.
