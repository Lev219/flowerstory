LATE GRADUATION ❤

The flowers I received for my best friend's high school graduation ceremony. My high school is in the United States and I missed my own graduation because of the pandemic. So I decided to attend my best friend's graduation ceremony to make up for my own graduation ceremony. It has always been my wish to graduate with my best friend. Although it was her graduation ceremony, she also prepared flowers for me. When I received the flowers, I felt very satisfied and happy because what I was missing, was fulfilled. For both of us, this is one of the most significant moments of our life, having the most important person accompanying each other.

2021年5月4日， 参加闺蜜高中毕业典礼收到的花。我的高中在美国，因为疫情原因，错过了自己的毕业典礼。于是我决定出席闺蜜的毕业典礼，也算是补过自己的毕业典礼。与闺蜜一起毕业一直都是我的心愿。虽然是参加她的毕业典礼，但是她也为我准备了花。收到花的时候，我觉得很圆满和幸福，因为我所缺失的，都得到了满足。对于我们两个人来说，这个人生最重要的瞬间之一，有最重要的人陪伴。
