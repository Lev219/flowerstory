LONG-DISTENCE ROMANCE

This was given to me by my boyfriend on Valentine's Day. Because of his pollen allergy, we usually don't have the habit of sending flowers to each other. We spend Valentine's Day together every year, but because of this year's long-distance relationship and different course schedules, we couldn't get together. Unexpectedly, he secretly ordered a bouquet of flowers for me. I was surprised, but what girl would not be happy to receive flowers? I was really excited and happy.

这是我男朋友在情人节送我的。因为他花粉过敏，我们平时没有互相送花的习惯。每年情人节我们都是在一起过的，但因为今年的异地恋，课程安排不同，我们无法相聚在一起。出乎意料的是，他悄悄为我订了束花。我很惊讶，但哪个女孩子收到花会不开心呢？真的会很激动很开心。
