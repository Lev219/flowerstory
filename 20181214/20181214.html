AFTER LOCKDOWN

For the first time in my life, I acted in a play and was one of the main characters. At the curtain call, I received a flower from the son of my homestay family. He went to the flower store and picked them out for me because I like purple. When I came out of the backstage for the curtain call, there was a lot of applause and the grandfather of the homestay family shouted:"GoGoGo!", and I saw a little boy running towards me with the flowers, he jumped on me and I hugged him and finished the curtain call with the flowers.

人生第一次演戏剧且作为主角之一，谢幕的时候收到住家小弟送给我的花。他专门去花店给我挑的，因为我喜欢紫色。在我从后台从出来谢幕，当时掌声一片，住家爷爷喊着gogogo, 看到一个小男孩拿着花朝我飞奔过来，他跳到我身上，我抱着他和花一起走完的谢幕仪式。
