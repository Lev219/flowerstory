GRADUATION DAY

Graduated from high school ！！！！ This is the happiest day of my 19 years old year!!! Not only because I got Parsons offer, but even more because I finally ended my high school life. Because I had to take a lot of classes that I wasn't interested in before I went to college. For example, math. It didn't make me feel happy very much of the time.

But it all ended on May 5th. At the graduation ceremony, I received a super nice bouquet from my mom. I loved the bouquet so much. My mom even chose the main color of the bouquet based on the color of my school uniform. It was so beautiful to end 3 years of high school with a bouquet of flowers.

高中毕业了！！！！这是我19岁那年最开心的一天！！不仅是因为我考上了我心仪的大学，更加是因为我终于结束了我的高中生活。因为在上大学前，我必须要学很多我不感兴趣的课程。比如数学。让我很时候并不觉得快乐。

但这一切都在5/5号那天结束了。毕业典礼上，我收到了妈妈送来的超好看的花束。我真的太喜欢这束花了。妈妈还特地根据我校服的颜色来选择花束主色调。以一束花来结尾高中3年，是多么美丽的一件事。
