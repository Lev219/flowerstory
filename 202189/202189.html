CALM AND ELEGANT

People are full of negative emotions when they lovelorn. For a long time I felt that it was my fault that the two were separated. I locked myself at home and stopped interacting with the outside world...but that was not a permanent solution. I felt it was time for a change when my body started to feel unwell on a regular basis. There after a period of settling down I realized that instead of pinning my fantasies and expectations on men I should make myself better. Putting the focus on yourself is really living in the present moment. It's not about looking forward to the future, nor is it about remembering the past. This bouquet of flowers is for myself. Use it to encourage yourself. Be the best version of myself. For the first time, I chose blue as the main color. I think what I want in my heart is the same as this bouquet - calm and elegant.
