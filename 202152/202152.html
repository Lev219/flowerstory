⚘ PINK PEONY ⚘

In our family, I spend all the festivals about love with my mom. This is actually one of my favorite bouquet out of all the flowers I gave to my mom. Both my mom and I love peony. Peony in ancient China expresses the feelings of longing of men and women Of course I do not have this emotion for my mother, we are simply attracted by the elegant appearance of peony.

2021年5月4日， 在我们家，所有关于爱情的节日我都会和妈妈一起度过。这其实是我送给妈妈那么多花里最喜欢的一束。我和妈妈都很喜欢芍药。芍药在中国古代表达的是男女相思之情：依依不舍，难舍难分。当然我对母亲没有这份情感，我们只是单纯的被芍药优雅的外表所吸引。
