LEGO SUPRISE ♡

At that time, I was lockdown in Shanghai for three months, and basically all my life came to a halt. I had to go to Guangzhou to get a visa because I had to go back to the US in July to continue my studies. I was questioned in the community, on the street, and even by the police. I drove 15 hours to Guangzhou with my boyfriend. In Guangzhou, we had to go through a 7+7 quarantine, just because we were from Shanghai. It was a coincidence that my 22nd birthday escaped the Shanghai lockdown but still did not escape the Guangzhou quarantine, spending my birthday in isolation for the first time. Perhaps because three or four months did not go out, did not eat a rich variety of take-out, perhaps three or four months did not see flowers. I was always very picky about flowers, but I received a delivery from my boyfriend on my birthday, probably from a random flower store around the neighborhood, and I felt so happy. I may not have been able to find a vase to grow it up in quarantine, but it felt like life was good again.

当时在上海经历了三个月封控，基本所有的生活都停摆了。因为7月份要回美国继续读书，所以只能不得已特地去去广州办签证。在社区，街道，甚至在警察重重盘问之后最终得以出发。我跟男朋友开了15小时的车去了广州。在广州要在进行7+7的隔离，只因为我们是上海来的。很不凑巧的是我22岁的生日逃过了上海lockdown依旧没有逃过广州的quarantine，第一次在隔离中度过了自己的生日。也许是因为三四个月没出过门，没吃过一顿种类丰富的外卖，也许是三四个月没见过鲜花。本来对花一向很挑剔的我在生日时收到了男朋友可能是随便找了一家周围花店的delivery，也感觉很幸福。可能我没有办法在隔离中找花瓶给他养起来，但是感觉生活又美好了起来。
