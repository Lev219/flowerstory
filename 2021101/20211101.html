"SENSE OF RITUAL"

Behind this bouquet of flowers is a bit of a tidbit. The thing is this: 10.1 day is my boyfriend's anniversary with me, it happens that 2020 is our 3 years together. It is reasonable that 10.1 is a day to have big celebration. Then he found that the flower store 10.1-10.4 bouquets were all booked out, and the rest of the bouquets were priced very high because of the holiday. Therefore, on 10.1, he first sent me a few red roses - to satisfy the ceremony of the day. Then on 10.5, he went and ordered a fancy bouquet for me. I don't quite remember how I felt at the time. We had agreed to do this as we are both very Pay attention to the sense of ritual.
